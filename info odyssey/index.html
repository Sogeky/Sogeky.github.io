ODYSSEY

Destiny

Zephyria Prime, in Orion, boasts floating crystals, bioluminescent life, and futuristic cities. Its elliptical orbit leads to extreme seasons, especially during the Lights season. Celestia Outpost attracts explorers for rare minerals, but colonization challenges arise from unpredictable atmospheres. Ongoing exploration targets Zephyria Prime's mysteries, promising groundbreaking intergalactic discoveries.

Position: Fourth planet from the Sun

Location: Andromeda, 9.461 trillion kilometers away from Earth

Size: Diameter of about 8,749 kilometers

Atmosphere: Carbon dioxide, Nitrogen, Argon, Oxygen
