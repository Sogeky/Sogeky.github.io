MOON

Destiny

The Moon, Earth's only natural satellite, has been a silent companion in the cosmos for around 4.5 billion years. Located at an average distance of 238,855 miles, it graces our night sky with its gentle glow, courtesy of reflected sunlight. The Moon's gravitational dance with Earth not only creates the ebb and flow of tides but also stabilizes our planet's axial tilt, influencing the rhythm of life on Earth. It has witnessed the passage of time and, through human exploration, has become a symbol of both mystery and scientific discovery.

Position: Natural sattelite of Earth

Location: Approximately 384,400 kms away from Earth

Size: Diameter of about 3,474 kilometers

Atmosphere: none
